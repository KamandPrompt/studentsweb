Student Review

I joined IIT Mandi for a Master of Technology in Mechanical Engineering. The high mountains and rivers surrounding the campus immediately made me feel at home. The friendliness and hospitality found in the local community of Mandi district was a beautiful experience. Despite being an international student, I never felt difficulties in coping up with academic and administrative activities. Being a part of a growing institution had an added advantage of easy access to Professors, administration and research facilities whilst experiencing a dynamic culture of colleagues coming from diverse backgrounds.

" To me, IIT Mandi is a portrayal of scenic beauty combined with academic excellence."

  • Prakash Giri, (MTech student at IIT Mandi, Aug. 2018-July 2020, home country: Nepal)

Student Review

My one month research internship in IIT Mandi is the experience I will always remember. IIT Mandi is a place that gives the impression of natural beauty and peacefulness as it is surrounded by mountains of Himalayas. The Institute director, faculty advisors, International students and relation office staff and everyone I encountered during my stay in the campus (including in my hostel and mess) were very helpful and friendly. I am very grateful to IIT Mandi for giving me the opportunity to spend one month and do my research in this amazing place with amazing people.

"I have learned a lot both academically and socially from the people I met every day."

  • Surafel Kindu, (3rd Year Software Engineering Student at Addis Ababa University, Ethiopia)
