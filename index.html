About IIT Mandi

Situated on the banks of Uhl river, IIT Mandi stands tall as a pillar of excellence combining the beautiful location of Kamand with its technological prowess creating an institution where brilliant creative people come together to learn, work and play. With a campus spread across an area of 538 acres, IIT Mandi has put up a formidable campus to nurture and nourish the future of this country.

A Glimpse of Students Life

IIT Mandi also pays special attention to an active campus life. As a result we can see a community of students and teachers who share common interests and make the student life memorable. From multiple sports comprising football, cricket, swimming , badminton etc to the student clubs covering almost every cultural and technical aspect, IIT Mandi also develops separate skill sets other than the course work which turn out to be valuable life lessons.
