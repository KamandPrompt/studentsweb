Student Life

IIT Mandi

Student life at IIT Mandi is cited by our alumni as the highlights of their college life memories. We at IIT Mandi advocate a healthy balance between education and extracurricular activities to guide our students to new levels and echo their leadership skills and talents through various academic workshops, co-curricular and extracurricular activities. Exodia: intercollegiate tech-cult fest, Rann-Neeti: intercollegiate sports fest, Astrax: intercollegiate astronomy fest ... ,Ruvaan: Intercollegiate lit fest, Utkarsh: Intra collegiate tech fest and Kala: Intra collegiate art fest, welcomes and empowers our students to celebrate and use their knowledge in a variety of endeavours. Competition club activities throughout the year enhance the talent of our students in several ways. IIT Mandi's various clubs welcome students to challenge themselves and discover and develop new skills, from technology, programming, debate, dance, theatre, music and more. IIT Mandi's dedicated student team also undertakes annual trips that provide our students with exhilarating experiences from tropical hills to snow-capped mountains. read more
